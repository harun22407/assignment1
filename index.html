About New York :


New York is composed of five boroughs - Brooklyn, the Bronx, Manhattan, Queens and Staten Island - is home to 8.4 million people who speak more than 200 languages, hail from every corner of the globe, and, together, are the heart and soul of the most dynamic city in the world. New York City is a global leader in industries ranging from technology to transportation to financial services to fashion to health care to education. More than 52 million people visit NYC each year to experience the city's unparalleled cultural institutions, visit our world-renowned parks, and dine at our more than 24,000 restaurants.
